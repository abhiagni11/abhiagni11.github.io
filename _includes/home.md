### Hi there, I'm Abhijeet!

I am studying robotics in beautiful Oregon. Born in 'Misrata', Libya and brought up in the pink city 'Jaipur' of India. I always had a fascination towards robots. From building legos to playing video games, I was trying to create new things and explore the world of imagination. Most of that is still true today...

Inspired from Andrej Karpathy's [blog](http://karpathy.github.io/), this place on the web would express musings of an aspiring roboticist!
